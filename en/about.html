Why does SENSFLOW exist?

Competitive players who move between several titles always hit the same problem: the sensitivity that works in CS2 is not the same in Valorant, in Apex or in Fortnite.

Most "calculators" out there simply multiply by a fixed factor, ignoring that every game has a different internal yaw value — the actual number of degrees the camera turns per mouse count.

Our tools use the correct formula: they compute the physical distance in centimeters for a 360° turn and convert that value precisely to any game.

How we handle data

All settings (profile, sensitivity, ADS multiplier) are stored exclusively in your browser, via localStorage.

We don't collect, send or store any of your data on servers. SENSFLOW works fully offline after the initial page load.
