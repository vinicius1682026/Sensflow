Quick summary

TL;DR: SENSFLOW does not collect your personally identifiable data. Your settings are saved only in your own browser. The site may display third-party ads, but we don't share personal data with advertisers nor build user profiles for advertising purposes.

This page exists to be transparent about how the site works technically and what data, if any, passes through our systems.

What we collect

SENSFLOW's tools run directly in your browser. That means we don't process your sensitivity data on our own servers — calculations happen locally, on your device.

We do not collect:

  • Name, email or any identifying data
  • Sensitivity settings or profiles sent to servers
  • Conversion history or tool usage
  • IP address (beyond what any web server automatically records in temporary logs)

Local data (localStorage)

The "My Universal Sensitivity" and "My Universal ADS" features use your browser's localStorage API to save preferences.

This means:

  • The data stays exclusively on your device
  • It is never transmitted to any server
  • You can delete it anytime using the "Clear" button on the panel, or by clearing site data in your browser settings
  • It is not shared across different devices

The stored keys are: sensflow_profile (hipfire sensitivity) and sensflow_ads_profile (ADS settings).

Third-party services

The site loads the following external resources:

  • Google Fonts — to load the Rajdhani, Inter and JetBrains Mono typefaces. Google may log that your IP made a request to its font servers. See Google's policy for more details.

The site may use third-party ad networks (such as Google AdSense) to display advertising. These networks may set their own cookies to personalize ads based on previous visits. You can manage these preferences in your browser settings or via ad opt-out.

We do not use Google Analytics or Meta Pixel to track user behavior.

Cookies

SENSFLOW does not use cookies. No cookie is set by the site. Preferences are saved via localStorage, which is technically different from cookies and is not automatically sent in HTTP requests.

Your rights

Since we don't collect personally identifiable data, there is no profile of yours to access, correct or delete in our systems.

To delete the data saved locally in your browser:

  • Use the "Clear" buttons on the site's profile panels
  • Or go to your browser settings → Privacy → Site data → SENSFLOW → Delete
