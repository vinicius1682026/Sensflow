Frequently asked questions

Yaw is how many degrees the camera turns per mouse count. Each engine uses a different value — for example, 0.022 on Source (CS2, Apex) and 0.07 in Valorant. That number is what makes a conversion accurate instead of a generic multiplier.

It's the physical distance, in centimetres, you need to move your mouse to turn 360° in game. Because it's a real-world measure, it stays the same across titles — which is why we use it as the basis for conversion.

eDPI is effective sensitivity: DPI × in-game sensitivity. It lets you compare setups between players using different DPI. Comparing eDPI only makes sense within the same game.
